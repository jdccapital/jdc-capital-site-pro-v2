Pre‑Export Finance

  1. Export/purchase contract between Producer and Offtaker assigned to Investors; Offtaker payments credited to Investors.
  2. Investors disburse USD loan to Producer and report to Central Bank.
  3. Through a Local Bank, Producer converts USD to local currency to operate.
  4. After export, Offtaker pays sale proceeds directly to Investors’ account abroad.

Import Finance

  1. Central Bank allows Investor disbursement to Supplier abroad (cash or advance against docs).
  2. Producer operates under Import Payment Monitoring via local FI.
  3. At maturity, Central Bank authorizes USD transfer abroad to Investors via local FI.

Investment Finance

  • Export‑oriented Project Finance / CapEx tailored to needs.
  • Structures aligned with Central Bank standards; efficient medium/long‑term financing with repayment abroad.
  • Debt refinancing applying export revenues abroad to optimize balance sheet.
