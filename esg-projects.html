ESG Projects

Energy Transition · Sustainability · Infrastructure · Health & Education · Agribusiness · Equity

Energy Transition

Renewable energy projects powered by new technologies (Solar, Wind, Hydro, Biogas & Biomass, Minerals & Metals for the transition, among others).

Sustainability

Efficiency upgrades in plants/equipment and processes to reduce ecological footprint: energy consumption, waste minimization, biodiversity protection, carbon footprint reduction, and resource optimization.

Infrastructure

Projects with positive social & ecological impact enabling access to efficient/sustainable services: Energy (incl. rural/off‑grid), Water & Sanitation, Transport (air/sea/land), Telecom & Interconnectivity.

Health & Education

Financing equipment & technology, R&D, staff training, and access to quality public‑private education.

Agribusiness

Green/organic crop production (e.g., sugarcane ethanol, sugar, citrus, fertilizers) and energy‑efficiency improvements in processing plants (soy, corn, wheat, etc.).

Equity

Funding initiatives that provide equal access to essential services and opportunities for the whole population.
